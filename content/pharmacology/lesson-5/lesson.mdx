# Pharmacology Module Exam

This comprehensive exam covers **all content from Lessons 1 through 4**. You must answer all 100 questions before submitting.

- **Lesson 2** — Calculating Drug Doses, Bronchodilators & Mucus-Controlling Therapy: solute/solvent/solution, drug dose calculations, SABAs, LABAs, anticholinergics, peak flow monitoring, xanthines, mucolytics

- **Lesson 4** — Pain Management, Vasopressors, Inotropes & Antiarrhythmic Agents: analgesics, sedatives, benzodiazepines, reversal agents, cardiac medications
