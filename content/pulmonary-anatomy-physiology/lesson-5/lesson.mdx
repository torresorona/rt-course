# Pulmonary Anatomy & Physiology Module Exam

This comprehensive exam covers **all content from Lessons 1 through 4**. You must answer all 50 questions before submitting.

## Topics Covered

- **Lesson 1 — Structure and Functional Dynamics of the Respiratory System:** upper and lower respiratory anatomy, conducting vs respiratory zones, epithelial types and PCCE, thoracic cavity and lungs, respiratory zone and pulmonary blood flow, pulmonary ventilation, lung volumes and capacities
- **Lesson 2 — Gas Exchange:** energy and temperature, gas pressure, volume and weight, ideal gas laws, gas transportation, gas diffusion, external and internal gas exchange
- **Lesson 3 — Regulation of Breathing:** medullary rhythmicity center, pneumotaxic and apneustic centers, central and peripheral chemoreceptors, proprioceptors
- **Lesson 4 — Acid Base Regulation:** hydrolysis equation, pH regulation, CO2 and HCO3- balance, protein buffer system, carbonic acid-bicarbonate buffer system

Good luck!
